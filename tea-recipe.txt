	Ingredients:
		- 3 teaspoons tea leaves
		- 1/4 cup water
		- 3/4 cup milk
		- sugar according taste

	Directions:
		1. Heat the water until it starts simmering. Add the tea leaves after it has achieved a gentle boil.

		2. Lower the flame and add the milk. Keep stirring the tea continously and raise the ladle in air to let some air in the tea to make it frothy

		3. Switch of the flame when it has achieved a boil.

		4. Add sugar as you please and your tea is now ready.
